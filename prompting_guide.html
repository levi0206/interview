Introduction

The OpenAI Cookbook offers important techniques for better GPT-4 performance. Here’s a quick summary:

Principles of Prompting

  • Be specific: Clearly describe what you want.
  • Provide context: Give background information.
  • Specify output format: Say if you want a list, JSON, table, etc.

Prompting Techniques

  • Instruction-based prompting: Direct instructions for the model.
  • Chain-of-Thought prompting: Ask the model to reason step-by-step.
  • Few-shot prompting: Provide examples to show the expected pattern.
  • System messages: Use special messages to define model behavior.

Tips for Better Results

  • Test and refine prompts iteratively.
  • Be explicit about assumptions and constraints.
  • Control output verbosity or structure through clear instructions.
